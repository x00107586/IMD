3rd Year Project

“The design and implementation of a platform video game”

For my most recent project assignment my team and I decided to research and develop a platform video game using Unity 5.3 engine. We began by researching past video games and moulding the best aspects of older classic video games with some ideas of our own. We decided to write the code in C# which we learned ourselves while creating the game. We also created a database so players could save their scores and compete against friends.

2nd Year Project

“The design and implementation of a part-time student registration system”

Research based project in which myself and my team examined the requirements for creating a part time registration system from the perspective of a student applying for a course, and also from the perspective of a member of the college administration. We then wrote the code and created the database to implement the system. Methodology included conducting interviews with the head of part-time student registration in ITT and researching the current process of enrolling in a part time course.
